### Overview
Built a quantitative assessment system, solvency modeling, and corporate valuation for Vietnam Airlines (HVN) using Python Streamlit.

### Detailed Implementation
- **Problem**: Needed a comprehensive tool to track and forecast HVN's complex financial situation, especially liquidity and bankruptcy risks.
- **Quantitative Assessment**: Applied advanced financial models (Altman Z''-Score, Beneish M-Score, and Sloan Accruals) to detect bankruptcy risk and earnings manipulation.
- **Solvency Modeling**: Built a Liquidity Runway and DSCR assessment framework to measure survival capability in liquidity stress scenarios.
- **Valuation & Stress Testing**: Performed valuation using DCF and EV Multiples. Integrated macroeconomic Stress Testing (assessing sensitivity to FX exchange rates and fuel costs).

### Results & Impact
- **Quantitative**: Built a dashboard to analyze 15 years of historical data (2011-2025) with high precision.
- **Qualitative**: Provided an intuitive, comprehensive view and early warning of corporate financial health, facilitating investor decision-making.

### Reflections & Learnings
- **Learnings**: Significantly improved Streamlit coding skills and corporate finance knowledge.
- **Challenges Overcome**: Handled noisy data and deployed complex DCF models to a web app.

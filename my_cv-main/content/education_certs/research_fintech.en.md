**Role**: Researcher  
**Timeframe**: 10/2023 – 04/2024  

### Overview
Researched the impact of the Fintech ecosystem on Financial Inclusion in the top 20 GDP-leading provinces in Vietnam.

### Detailed Implementation
- **Problem**: Quantitative assessment of the impact of financial technology on people's access to financial services.
- **Architecture & Solutions**:
  - Used Stata 17 software for data analysis.
  - Analyzed current barriers: low R&D investment (<0.5% GDP), uneven infrastructure.
  - Ran regression models to measure influencing factors.

### Results & Impact
- **Quantitative**: Identified the Fintech ecosystem as the main influencing factor (GLS regression coefficient is 7.56, statistically significant at the 1% level).
- **Qualitative**: Forecasted that by 2026, the two international economic centers (HCMC & Da Nang) will reduce 2/3 of the barriers.

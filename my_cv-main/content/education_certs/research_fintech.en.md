### 1. Overview
Researched the impact of the Fintech ecosystem on Financial Inclusion across the top 20 GDP-leading provinces in Vietnam from 2017 to 2023.

### 2. Methodology & Research Process
- **Research Goal**: Quantify and scientifically evaluate the impact of financial technology on access to financial services, identifying structural hurdles.
- **Methodology & Models**:
  - Implemented panel data regression analysis on **Stata 17**.
  - Evaluated OLS (Ordinary Least Squares), REM (Random Effects Model), and FEM (Fixed Effects Model).
  - Addressed model specification defects and corrected them using General Least Squares (GLS) estimation.

### 3. Key Results & Impact
- **Quantitative**: Identified the Fintech ecosystem as the explanatory variable with the strongest positive impact on the financial inclusion index, showing a regression coefficient of $GLS = 7.56$ (highly statistically significant at the $1\%$ level, $p < 0.01$). Identified structural bottlenecks: low national R&D investment ($< 0.5\% \text{ GDP}$) and regional technological infrastructure asymmetry.
- **Qualitative**: Modeled technological barrier transitions, forecasting that the two largest international economic centers (HCMC and Da Nang) will reduce $2/3$ of structural technological barriers by 2026.

### 4. Reflections & Learnings
- **Learnings**: Mastered academic quantitative research logic, panel data processing, and econometric specification testing to derive robust empirical conclusions.
- **Challenges Overcome**: Resolved autocorrelation and heteroscedasticity problems within the panel dataset via diagnostic testing and GLS estimation adjustments.

### 1. Overview
Conducted econometric research on how fintech access drives financial inclusion across 20 Vietnamese provinces from 2017 to 2023.

### 2. Methodology & Research Process
- **Research Goal**: Quantify and scientifically evaluate the impact of financial technology on access to financial services, identifying structural hurdles.
- **Methodology & Models**:
  - Implemented panel data regression analysis on **Stata 17**.
  - Evaluated OLS (Ordinary Least Squares), REM (Random Effects Model), and FEM (Fixed Effects Model).
  - Applied Feasible Generalized Least Squares (FGLS) to handle heteroscedasticity and autocorrelation, ensuring robust statistical inference on alternative digital finance indicators.

### 3. Key Results & Impact
- **Quantitative**: Identified the Fintech ecosystem as the explanatory variable with the strongest positive impact on the financial inclusion index, showing a regression coefficient of $GLS = 7.56$ (highly statistically significant at the $1\%$ level, $p < 0.01$). This directly aligns with MoMo's mission of serving underbanked populations.
- **Qualitative**: Modeled technological barrier transitions, forecasting that major economic centers will reduce structural technological barriers.

### 4. Reflections & Learnings
- **Learnings**: Mastered academic quantitative research logic, panel data processing, and econometric specification testing to derive robust empirical conclusions.
- **Challenges Overcome**: Resolved autocorrelation and heteroscedasticity problems within the panel dataset via diagnostic testing and FGLS estimation adjustments.
